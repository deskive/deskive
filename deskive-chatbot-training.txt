ABOUT DESKIVE
-------------
Deskive is an all-in-one workspace platform that brings together everything your team needs to collaborate, communicate, and get work done. It combines six core modules - Chat, Video Calls, Projects, Files, Calendar, and Notes - plus advanced AI-powered features, email integration, collaborative whiteboard, analytics, and much more into a single unified platform.

Deskive replaces fragmented tools like Slack, Notion, Trello, Google Drive, Zoom, and others with one integrated solution designed for modern teams. Key highlights include:

- Real-time team messaging with channels, threads, and direct messages
- HD video conferencing with screen sharing, recording, and real-time AI transcription
- Project management with Kanban boards, task tracking, and time management
- Secure cloud storage with file versioning and Google Drive integration
- Smart calendar with scheduling, meeting rooms, and availability checking
- Rich text notes with real-time collaboration and templates
- Full email integration with Gmail OAuth and SMTP/IMAP support
- Advanced AI features including AutoPilot AI Agent, Meeting Intelligence, and smart summaries
- Collaborative whiteboard for visual brainstorming
- Powerful analytics and reporting dashboard
- Cross-platform sync across web, desktop, and mobile

A: Deskive is available on Web (all modern browsers), Desktop (macOS and Windows), Android (Google Play Store), and iOS (App Store).

A: Deskive includes AutoPilot AI Agent, Meeting Intelligence with automatic summaries, Real-time Transcription during video calls, Document Analysis, Smart Summaries, Translation, Content Generation for notes and emails, and Smart Search across all content.

Q: How many team members can I have?
A: Free: 5 members. Starter: 25 members. Professional: 100 members. Enterprise: Unlimited members.

A: Free: 512 MB. Starter: 25 GB. Professional: 100 GB. Enterprise: 1 TB or more.

A: HD video conferencing, screen sharing, meeting recording, background blur, virtual backgrounds, public meeting links, real-time transcription, meeting intelligence with summaries, and action items extraction.

A: Yes! Deskive syncs in real-time across web, desktop, and mobile devices. Use it on your computer, phone, and tablet simultaneously.

A: AutoPilot is an autonomous AI assistant that can perform tasks on your behalf within Deskive. It can help with task creation, scheduling, note taking, and more based on natural language commands.

A: Meeting Intelligence automatically generates summaries of your video calls, extracts action items, and identifies key discussion points using AI.
